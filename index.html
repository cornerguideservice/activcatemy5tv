Get Started — Activate Now

Quick Summary

Use channel5.com/activate to link your device with your Channel 5 (My5) account. Works on Smart TVs, Roku, Fire Stick, Apple TV, PlayStation, Xbox, and mobile apps.

Step 1 — Open My5 App

Launch the My5 app on your device and navigate to the Sign In / Activate option.

Step 2 — Note the Activation Code

A unique code will appear on your screen. Keep this screen open until you complete activation.

Step 3 — Visit Channel5.com/activate

On your phone or computer, open channel5.com/activate, sign in, and enter the activation code.

Step 4 — Confirm & Stream

Once verified, your TV refreshes and you can enjoy all content from Channel 5 (My5).

How to Activate on Specific Devices

  • Roku: Install the My5 channel, open it, and follow on-screen instructions to generate your code.
  • Amazon Fire Stick: Download My5 from the Amazon App Store, open it, and activate using channel5.com/activate.
  • Apple TV: Use the App Store to install My5, sign in, and enter your code on the website.
  • PlayStation/Xbox: Download the app, sign in, and activate via the code displayed on the console.

Troubleshooting Common Problems

Expired Code

Generate a new code by restarting the My5 app and repeat the steps.

Login Errors

Ensure your My5 account credentials are correct or reset your password online.

App Crashing

Update or reinstall the app from your device’s store.

Geo-restrictions

My5 is available only in the UK. Access from outside may require a VPN.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to pay for activation?

No, activation at channel5.com/activate is free of charge. You only need a registered My5 account.

Can I use one account on multiple devices?

Yes, you can sign in and activate on multiple devices simultaneously.

Yes, many programs come with subtitles. You can enable them in the app settings.

Can I download shows offline?

Currently, offline downloads are not supported in the My5 app.
